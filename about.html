Who we are

Natural Storage Solutions Pvt. Ltd. (NSSPL) is a leading turnkey solution provider for all types of cold storage and food-processing units — covering fruits and vegetables, dairy, seafood, meat and more — serving customers from India to the globe.

We support our customers across every stage: conceptualization of the project, engineering and design, integration of technologies, manufacturing, execution and commissioning, and continued support during operation. Projects we execute are automatic, low in energy consumption, and highly efficient in operation.

Our facility

NSSPL operates a fully equipped manufacturing facility in a well-developed industrial zone near Ahmedabad, Gujarat. That in-house capability lets us keep quality, timelines and accountability under one roof — from the first drawing to the final commissioned plant.

Strategic associations

NSSPL maintains strategic associations with some of the world's leading companies for products such as insulated panels, ventilation technology and controlled-atmosphere machinery — bringing proven components into every turnkey system we build.

Our portfolio includes refrigeration plants for multi-purpose cold storage, controlled-atmosphere storage for fruits, specially designed storage for root vegetables, frozen plants with IQF and allied processing machinery, fruit-ripening rooms, pre-cooling rooms, and various types of ice plants and ice-conveying systems. We also offer cold-chain expertise with special-purpose food-processing equipment, including vegetable dryers.
