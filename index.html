Who we are

A single partner for the entire project lifecycle.

Natural Storage Solutions Pvt. Ltd. (NSSPL) is a turnkey solution provider for all types of cold storage and food-processing units — fruits and vegetables, dairy, seafood, meat and more. We stay with our customers from the first idea to a fully running plant.

  • Conceptualization and feasibility for every project
  • Engineering design and integration of proven technologies
  • In-house manufacturing at a facility near Ahmedabad, Gujarat
  • Execution, commissioning and ongoing operational support

Solutions across the full cold chain.
